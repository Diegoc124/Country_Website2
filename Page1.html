Chichen Itza is an ancient Mayan city in Mexico, known for the Pyramid of Kukulcán. A UNESCO World Heritage site, it features impressive ruins, including temples and observatories, and is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

The Tulum Ruins are ancient Mayan ruins located on a cliff overlooking the Caribbean Sea. Known for their well-preserved structures, including a walled city and the Temple of the Frescoes, Tulum offers a stunning mix of history and scenic views.
